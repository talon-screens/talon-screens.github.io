About Talon Screens

Premium Screen Manufacturing for
Dealers & Installers

Talon Screens is a specialized manufacturer of custom retractable screens and vinyl panels designed to elevate both residential and commercial outdoor living spaces. Built with precision and crafted for performance, our products combine innovation, durability, and aesthetic appeal—engineered to meet the demands of diverse climates and installation requirements across North America. Our expertise comes from over two decades in the screen industry, giving us unique insight into what installers need and customers expect.

FAQ

  • How do I become a Talon Screens dealer?

    Becoming a Talon Screens dealer is simple. Visit our "Become a Dealer" page and complete the application form. Our team will review your application and contact you to discuss next steps, including training, support, and ordering processes.

  • What dealer support does Talon Screens offer?

    We provide comprehensive dealer support including product training, marketing materials, technical support, installation guides, warranty support, and a dedicated dealer portal for ordering products and accessing resources. Our team is committed to helping you succeed with our products.

  • What is the ordering process for dealers?

    Approved dealers can place orders through our secure online dealer portal. Simply log in, select your products, specify customization options, and submit your order. Our team will process it quickly, and you'll receive updates on production and shipping status through the portal.

  • What customization options are available for your products?

    Talon Screens offers extensive customization options including various frame colors and finishes, multiple mesh types for different levels of visibility and insect protection, manual or motorized operation, smart home integration capabilities, and custom sizing to fit virtually any space.
