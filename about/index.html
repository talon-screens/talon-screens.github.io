OUR STORY

From Installation to Innovation

Two decades of field experience drives every product we manufacture

The Foundation

For over two decades, our parent company, Screens of Georgia, has been a leading provider of custom screens and outdoor living solutions throughout Georgia. We specialized in retractable and motorized screens, door screen systems, fixed screen enclosures, retractable awnings, security shutters, and solar shades.

Through thousands of installations, we developed deep relationships with top manufacturers like Sunesta, Insolroll, Mirage, QMI, and Genius. We learned what worked, what didn't, and where the industry needed better solutions.

22+ Years of Installation Experience

The Innovation

After years of working with various manufacturers, we recognized an opportunity to create products that truly met the needs of professional installers and their customers. We understood the frustrations, the quality issues, and the features that would make installations smoother and more reliable.

That's when Talon Screens was born - a manufacturing company built on the foundation of real-world installation experience, dedicated to producing superior retractable screens and vinyl panels for the professional market.

  • Comprehensive dealer support programs
  • Quality backed by decades of field experience
